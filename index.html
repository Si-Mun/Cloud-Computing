SiMun, Help mountaineer to prepare for hiking

A climbing planning application that makes it easy for climbers to find all information regarding education, equipment, preparation and anticipation. In general, this application provides information and tips about the mountain to be climbed, recommendations for items to bring, what needs to be prepared, then provides education to anticipate the risks of danger that will be faced during the climb and there is a camera feature that can detect items that must be carried. recommended, and optional. So that with this application it is hoped that it can minimize the risk of danger and unwanted things that occur during mountain climbing.

Education for mountaineers

Before going on a hike, it is very important to know what things need to be prepared both in terms of physical, mental and navigation knowledge which is very much needed when you are in nature. Here we provide some information that will help you to avoid the risk of harm or unwanted things from happening, namely by providing information in the form of causes, anticipation and appropriate treatment when these problems occur.

Image camera scaner

There is a camera scan feature that makes it easy to check which luggage is important and which is not. When the object is scanned it will display the name of the object along with its description, then provide a color code which if it comes out red then the item must be brought, orange is highly recommended to be brought, and green is optional to be brought. The results of the scan will provide information about the function of the item, the reason why it was taken, and provide a solution if the item is left at home or lost during a hike.

Suggestions equipment that is usually used for climbing

You must be confused, especially beginner climbers, what items do you need to bring when climbing? Here we provide some recommendations for items to bring while climbing the mountain, as well as provide an explanation of the function of these items, the reasons why these items are taken, and also provide solutions if these items are left at home or lost during the hike. So, for those of you who want to climb a mountain, for example, if you are constrained by the time you are required to leave tomorrow or when you are tight, here is a list of items to bring and provides color information for items that must be carried in red, orange is highly recommended, and green is optional. to bring.

Provide information about the mountain

Before climbing a mountain, of course, it is very important to know all the detailed information about the mountain to be climbed. So, here we have provided a summary of information about what is interesting about each mountain, the reasons you should climb the mountain, a total discussion of both registration and motorcycle taxi prices, tips on climbing the mountain, providing information on prohibitions that should not be violated, equipment suggestions and luggage that needs to be prepared, the risks that will occur and things that must be avoided, and of course what is most sought after is information about how many mountain trails there are and the estimated travel time from the bascamp to the top. Here we have provided information on the estimated average time from post to post so that you can easily predict your time in a good and structured manner.
